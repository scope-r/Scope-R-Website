Introducing Scope-R

Product News
  • February 29, 2020
  • by Guillaume Clement

Why Scope-R?

Estimation is hard. With so much at stake, so little time, and high expectations, the estimation process has been an inexact science for far too long. With requests for quotes coming in on a daily basis, it can be very difficult for professionals to feel confident about their estimates. On one hand, estimating too low means project failure; continuous change requests, lack of budget, and often time high tempers. On the other, estimating too high means you are likely to “price yourself out of the deal”.

With so much at stake, you deserve to be using the best tools possible to guarantee the success of your projects, and that is why we’re here!

What is it?

“Scope-R is an automated estimation tool created to help individuals and teams follow an assisted and unified scoping methodology.”

With Scope-R you don’t have to be a SCRUM Master (or even know what that means) to estimate work following the best concepts and methodology available. Using the assistive technology that Scope-R offers, you can ensure that you or your team never offers estimates on which you can’t deliver or at a price you can’t meet ever again!

How do we do it?

Using assistive technology presented in an easy to use and understand application we offer professionals a way to break down thoughts and ideas into categories and tasks by following a holistic approach and guiding them through a bottom-up estimation process.

By breaking down task-level details, assumptions, risk, and relative complexity, this process allows our intelligent algorithm to help users through the break-down process and to plan with confidence and efficiency.
